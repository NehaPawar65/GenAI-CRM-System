# GenAI-CRM-System

Product Scope 

The Generative AI-based Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system is designed to analyze customer data, predict behavior, and improve customer engagement using advanced machine learning and artificial intelligence techniques. The system integrates multiple modules such as data collection, preprocessing, customer segmentation, customer lifetime value estimation and recommendation systems. It also includes Generative AI capabilities for automated customer interaction through chatbots and personalized content generation, enabling organizations to provide efficient and intelligent customer service. 

The primary goal of the system is to transform traditional CRM into a predictive and automated platform that supports data-driven decision-making. By leveraging AI and NLP techniques, the system helps businesses identify valuable customers, optimize marketing strategies, and enhance overall customer satisfaction. The proposed system is scalable, adaptable, and suitable for modern business environments, providing a competitive advantage through improved operational efficiency and personalized customer experiences.
